Hey there! I'm a senior at the University of Toronto.

I am very fortunate to work with Khai Truong at the DGP Lab, and Devon Hjelm and Yoshua Bengio at MILA on cutting-edge research problems in Human-Computer Interaction and Deep Learning.

I want to recreate human intelligence. I think representation learning, the math behind learning in modern AI, and understanding natural language are key topics/problems that offer insights into how to further advance computational intelligence. So far, my work has been on improving deep generative models through improved representation learning and zero-shot generalization.

Previously, I spent a few months studying at the National University of Singapore. Feel free to get in touch! :-)
