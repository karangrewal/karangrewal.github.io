About me

Hey there! I'm a senior at the University of Toronto.


I am very fortunate to work with Khai Truong at the DGP Lab, and Devon Hjelm and Yoshua Bengio at MILA on cutting-edge research problems in Human-Computer Interaction and Deep Learning.


I'm interested in creating human-level intelligence through equipping AI with human-like cognitive behaviour. So far, my work has been on improving deep generative models through improved representation learning and zero-shot generalization.


Previously, I spent a few months studying at the National University of Singapore. Feel free to get in touch! :-)
