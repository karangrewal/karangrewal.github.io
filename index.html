Karan Grewal

Hey there! I'm a senior at the University of Toronto.

I am very fortunate to work with Khai Truong at the DGP Lab, and Devon Hjelm and Yoshua Bengio at MILA on cutting-edge research problems in Human-Computer Interaction and Deep Learning.

What principles underly intelligent behaviour? Using math, statistics, and representation learning, I want to model these phenomenon. These are essential tools that I believe will further advance computational intelligence. So far, my work has been on deep generative models through improving representation learning and zero-shot generalization.

Previously, I spent a few months studying at the National University of Singapore. Feel free to get in touch! Also, See my Curriculum Vitae for more information.
