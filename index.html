About

Hey there! I am a senior undergraduate student at the University of Toronto. I am very fortunate to work with Khai Truong at the DGP Lab, and Devon Hjelm and Yoshua Bengio at MILA.

As an undergrad, I have worked on training generative models, discovering rude behaviour through language and acoustic cues, and learning routine behaviour with topic models.

Feel free to send me an email to get in touch.
